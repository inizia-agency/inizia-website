How it works

At Inizia, we meet you where you are in your product journey — from idea to launch, and beyond. Each tier is a monthly, fixed-fee partnership. We don’t charge by the hour or scope features line-by-line. Instead, we work in sprints and retainers that align with your current goals — whether that’s validating an idea, building your MVP, stabilising a struggling product, or making ongoing improvements.

You can start, pause, upgrade, or switch tiers as your product evolves. Each tier includes clear deliverables, real collaboration, and dedicated time from our team to make progress on what matters most.

Validate

Three months to test your idea with real users — before you invest in code.

You’ve got the industry insight; we run structured experiments to confirm demand, shape the solution, and give you evidence to move forward confidently.

Who it’s for
  • Business owners stepping into tech who want clarity before building
  • Leaders needing proof for partners, boards or early backers
Benefits
  • Clear go/no-go backed by customer data — reduce risk
  • Prioritised feature set so you only build what matters
  • Clickable prototype to align teams and support pitches
  • Early audience capture via landing page, surveys and tests

Build

Your product team from MVP to traction. Launch, learn, iterate.

We design, code and ship your MVP in ~90 days, then adapt based on real usage. One flexible engagement that moves from MVP into iteration and monetisation without you juggling vendors.

Who it’s for
  • Non-technical founders who want an accountable, senior product team
  • Businesses ready to launch a first version and improve every month
Benefits
  • Fast path to a working product in customers’ hands
  • Measurable monthly progress and transparent releases
  • Lean scope control so spend tracks to outcomes
  • Technical foundation built to scale or hand over cleanly

Sustain

Keep momentum with small features, UX polish and steady improvements.

Your product is live and stable. Sustain keeps it fresh and useful with light adjustments, UX enhancements and fixes that improve retention — without the cost of a full build cycle.

Who it’s for
  • Teams post-launch who want continuous improvement at a lighter pace
  • Founders balancing go-to-market activity with product care
Benefits
  • Happier users via regular UX refinements
  • Reduced tech debt with ongoing fixes and updates
  • Clear monthly impact without heavy overhead
  • Easy switch back to Build when a bigger push is needed

Maintain

Peace of mind. Monitoring, security and updates handled for you.

We keep your app healthy behind the scenes so you can focus on the business. Monitoring, security patches, dependency updates and light content changes are covered.

Who it’s for
  • Products no longer in full build mode that still need reliable care
  • Owners who want zero-fuss tech upkeep
Benefits
  • Higher uptime and fewer emergencies
  • Security and package updates done on schedule
  • Clear monthly health reports and release logs
  • Lower long-term risk through preventative maintenance

Project Rescue

Short, high-intensity recovery for at-risk products.

When delivery stalls or quality slips, we step in fast. We audit, triage and stabilise the codebase, then ship against clear recovery milestones so you can regain control.

Who it’s for
  • Businesses inheriting broken builds or overdue projects
  • Leaders who need a trusted team to steady the ship quickly
Benefits
  • Clarity in two weeks with a practical recovery plan
  • Rapid risk reduction and restored stakeholder confidence
  • Codebase stabilised and ready for ongoing development
  • Smoother handover into Build, Sustain or your internal team
