How it works

At Inizia, we meet you where you are in your product journey — from idea to launch, and beyond. Each tier is a monthly, fixed-fee partnership. We don’t charge by the hour or scope features line-by-line. Instead, we work in sprints and retainers that align with your current goals — whether that’s validating an idea, building your MVP, stabilising a struggling product, or making ongoing improvements.

You can start, pause, upgrade, or switch tiers as your product evolves. Each tier includes clear deliverables, real collaboration, and dedicated time from our team to make progress on what matters most.

Validate

Run structured experiments to see if your idea is worth building.

You’ve got a product idea — but is it solving the right problem? Validate is our hands-on, low-risk approach to test the potential of your concept before investing in full development.

What’s included:
  • Mastermind workshop to shape your idea
  • Competitor, user, and market research
  • Landing page, survey, and ad campaign
  • Clickable prototype
  • Clear go/no-go report based on real insights

For example: Launching a new B2B app? We’ll test demand, interview users, and build a prototype that helps you pitch or plan with confidence.

Who is it for:

Founders who want clarity, insight, and confidence before committing to build.

Accelerate

Build fast with a dedicated product team at your side.

Ready to go live? Accelerate is your dedicated product team — designing, coding, releasing and iterating in fast, focused sprints. You stay focused on business. We ship the product.

What’s included:
  • UX/UI design & feature prioritisation
  • Full-stack development & infrastructure
  • Weekly updates + strategy workshops
  • Agile delivery & CI/CD setup
  • System documentation & handover if needed

For example: Building a booking app? We’ll map the flows, build the backend, integrate payments, and iterate toward a polished v1 release.

Who is it for:

Founders and teams ready to build, test, and release with speed and strategy.

Grow

Continuous small improvements to keep your product fresh.

Already launched, but not done? Grow is a flexible way to keep your product polished, useful, and performing — without jumping into another full build cycle.

What’s included:
  • Minor UX/UI improvements (up to 5/month)
  • Light feature adjustments (up to 3/month)
  • Bug fixes & support
  • Performance reports
  • Optional monthly strategy check-in

For example: Need to tweak form fields or clean up a mobile layout? We’ll keep it fresh and relevant without a full rebuild.

Who is it for:

Product owners seeking consistent polish, light improvements, and UX care.

Maintain

Ongoing monitoring, maintenance, and peace of mind.

Maintain is our behind-the-scenes support layer — quietly keeping your product secure, stable and running smoothly, so you don’t have to think about it.

What’s included:
  • Performance monitoring and health checks
  • Security updates and bug fixes
  • Quarterly package updates
  • Release tracking and reports
  • Light copy and visual adjustments

For example: We catch broken links, patch bugs, and keep your stack current — while you focus on growing your business.

Who is it for:

Founders and teams who want a reliable partner for ongoing care and stability.

Project Rescue

Stabilise and recover a product that’s gone off track.

Something’s not working — and time’s running out. Project Rescue is an intensive, short-term engagement to audit, triage, and stabilise a failing or stalled product.

What’s included:
  • Rapid codebase audit & findings report
  • Recovery & stabilisation plan
  • Critical fixes and UX rebuilds
  • Infrastructure, backend and frontend work
  • Project portal + coordination with your team

For example: Inheriting a broken app from another agency? We’ll step in, clean it up, and get it over the line — fast.

Who is it for:

Businesses with a high-stakes, high-pressure project that needs fast recovery.
